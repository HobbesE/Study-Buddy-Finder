About the Creator


Hobbes is a software engineer transplanted to San Francisco, CA from the Midwest. Topeka, Kansas and Kansas City, Missouri are two places Hobbes still calls "Home". Before starting to code at age 30, Hobbes worked as a community organizer for expanding rights, access, protection, and policy for working class families. Programming is an extension of this deeply relational work to Hobbes, and they are excited to use their full-stack programming skillset to continue building power within marginalized communities. Hobbes joined forces with Hackbright Academy as a student in 2021 amidst the COVID-19 pandemic, starting with the Python Prep Course before moving on to the Full-Time Remote Software Engineering Program, the sixth program to experience Hackbright Academy fully remotely.
Hackbrighter became a project within the first weeks of the remote program, as a way for learners to organize study spaces without the luxury of having a physical hub. It became Hobbes' capstone project for the program, and was deployed in July 2021 as a space for strengthening the social network of gender diverse students in technology across the world.

About Hackbright Academy


Hackbright Academy is a coding bootcamp celebrated for empowering gender diverse programmers to change the gender ratio in technology. Hackbright offers a five week python course for those interested in getting their feet wet in programming, along with an intensive full-stack software engineering bootcamp that provides students with web development skillsets to create web apps like this one... and then some!
